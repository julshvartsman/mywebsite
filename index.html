About Me

I'm a designer and developer with over 5 years of experience creating engaging digital products. My approach combines aesthetic sensibility with technical expertise to build solutions that are both beautiful and functional.

After completing my degree in Design & Technology at New York University, I worked with several startups and established companies to develop their digital presence and product offerings.

My philosophy centers on user-centric design that balances innovation with clarity. I believe the most successful digital experiences feel intuitive while still offering moments of delight.

Outside of my professional work, I stay current with industry developments, contribute to open-source projects, and enjoy landscape photography.
